About me

I am currently PhD candidate (on the last year) jointly at the ETIS lab and at the Paris Seine Université (Université de Cergy-Pontoise), France, since 2015, under the supervision of David Picard and Hedi Tabia. I am also supported by the Brazilian National Council for Scientific and Technological Development (CNPq). I received my Master's degree on Applied Computing (Graphics and Computer Vision) from PPGCA, Federal University of Technology - Paraná (UTFPR), Brazil, in 2015, and my Bachelor's degree in Electronic Engineering from UTFPR, in 2011. In between, I have worked in the industry with computer vision applied to embedded and real time systems.

Research interests

My research interests are mainly on computer vision and machine learning, specially applied to 3D scenes and to human behaviour understanding.
